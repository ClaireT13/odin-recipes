Rice Krispies Treat

When I was 11 or 12, I remember going to a birthday party that was held in a cooking studio.

That was where I learnt to make these sugar-loaded chewy treats.

Ingredients

  • 150g Rice Krispies Cereal
  • 3 Tablespoons Salted Butter
  • 300g Marshmallows

Method

  1. Grease a 20cm x 30cm pan very well.
  2. Melt butter in a large pot over low heat.
  3. Pour marshmallows into pot and melt over low heat. Stir occasionaly to prevent burning.
  4. When the marmallows have turned runny (like a thick batter), pour in the rice krispies treat and mix well.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pan and flatten with the back of damp spoon.
  6. Let it cool completely before cutting it into squares.
