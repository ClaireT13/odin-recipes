Tzatziki

We all enjoy a good sauce.

This tzatziki is perfect for cutting through the grease and revivng your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • 1 english cucumber, deseeded
  • 1 clove of garlic, ground to a paste
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup greek yoghurt
  • salt

Method

  1. Finely grate the cucumber and salt it well.
  2. Mix well and let it sit for 20 minutes.
  3. Rinse the cucumber well and strain it through a cheesecloth.
  4. Use your hands to separate the cucumber bits.
  5. Add the garlic paste, lemon juice, and greek yoghurt to the cucumber and mix well.
  6. Add salt to taste and serve.
